# Authority boundaries

MAMV-Model emits answer text, source selection, inference frames, reasoning summaries, confidence signals, genericity heuristics, and explicitly non-authoritative claim, evidence, and relation candidates. It never emits a truth assertion, final verdict, trust receipt, receipt hash, signature, or public verification status.

MAMV owns claim normalization, evidence verification, quorum and verdict policy, and trust-receipt creation, hashing, signing, and publication. MAMV-IR owns workflow state, observer authority, acceptance criteria, repair loops, constitutional review, and completion decisions.
